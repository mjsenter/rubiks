At start of program: Input how many pieces you want (nxn) ex: 3x3, 4x4, etc.

OBJECTS:
8 Corner pieces
6* (Number of sides - 2)^2 Center pieces
12*(Number of sides - 2) Edge pieces
7 colors

CONTROLS:
Use a cursor approach to select which layer/row you want to rotate.
V, v - Rotate vertically
H, h - Rotate horizontally
Arrow keys Move the Cursor
WASD keys rotate entire cube
Q - Quit
R - reset
O - Scramble

Give feedback if finished.
